# School AI Policy Checklist

Use this checklist before introducing AI tools to students, teachers, or administrative teams.

## 1. Define approved use
- State what AI may be used for.
- State what AI must not be used for.
- Separate student, teacher, and admin use cases.

## 2. Protect students
- Do not allow students to submit sensitive personal information into AI tools.
- Require teacher supervision for younger learners.
- Define age-appropriate AI activities.

## 3. Protect academic integrity
- Explain the difference between AI assistance and cheating.
- Require students to show their thinking and process.
- Use oral checks, drafts, and project reviews.

## 4. Train teachers first
- Run teacher orientation before student rollout.
- Provide sample prompts for lesson planning, feedback, and revision.
- Agree on consistent classroom rules.

## 5. Communicate with parents
- Explain why the school is introducing AI.
- Explain safety controls.
- Give parents a simple guide for home supervision.

## 6. Review every term
- Collect teacher feedback.
- Review student outcomes.
- Update policy as tools and risks change.
