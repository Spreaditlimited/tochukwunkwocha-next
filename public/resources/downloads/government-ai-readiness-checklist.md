# Government AI Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist to assess whether a public institution is ready to adopt AI responsibly.

## Service delivery
- Identify services with long queues, repeated questions, or manual paperwork.
- Prioritize use cases that improve citizen experience.
- Avoid starting with sensitive or high-risk decisions.

## Data readiness
- Know what data exists.
- Know who owns the data.
- Remove unnecessary personal information before experimentation.

## Staff capacity
- Train staff on practical AI use.
- Create internal guidelines.
- Appoint responsible AI champions in each department.

## Governance
- Define approval processes for AI tools.
- Record what tools are used and for what purpose.
- Review outputs before they affect citizens.

## Public trust
- Be transparent about AI-assisted services.
- Provide human escalation channels.
- Monitor errors, complaints, and bias risks.

## First pilot
Choose one low-risk process where AI can reduce time, improve clarity, or support staff productivity.
