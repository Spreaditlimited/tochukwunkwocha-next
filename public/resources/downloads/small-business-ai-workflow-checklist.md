# Small Business AI Workflow Checklist

Use this checklist to identify practical places where AI can save time in a Nigerian small business.

## Customer communication
- Draft WhatsApp replies.
- Turn customer complaints into professional responses.
- Create follow-up messages after purchase.

## Sales and marketing
- Write product descriptions.
- Create weekly social media post ideas.
- Turn customer questions into content.

## Operations
- Build simple task checklists.
- Summarize supplier conversations.
- Create standard operating procedures.

## Finance and records
- Categorize expenses.
- Summarize sales notes.
- Prepare questions for an accountant.

## Hiring and training
- Draft role descriptions.
- Create onboarding checklists.
- Build simple training notes.

## Rule
Start with one repeated task. Improve it with AI before adding another workflow.
